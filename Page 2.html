MatPat(Matthew Patrick) from the Theorist channels

Matthew Patrick or MatPat is a well-known YouTuber famous for making games, movies and TV shows interesting by theorising about different plot holes, unsolved mysteries and references. He, across all his channels on YouTube, has over 35 million subscribers!

Year Achievement
2018 Reached 10 million subscribers
2018 Raised $5 million for charity on a LIVE stream
2020 Started the new channel 'Food Theory' which was massive hit
2021 Campainging to be the guest host of the show 'Jeopardy'
