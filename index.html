J.K.Rowling

Author of the Harry Potter book series
Millionaire

Joanne Rowling, better known by her pen name J.K. Rowling, is a British children's book author and poet. Her book series Harry Potter has broken all records and is loved by kids and teens around the world. She is also the first person to become a millionaire by writing books. She currently runs the website PotterMore where she gives fans an insight into the Wizarding World, reveals more about fan-favourite characters and solves mysteries which were left out of the canon books and movies.
